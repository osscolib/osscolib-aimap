 
 Using osscolib-atomichash from Maven 2+
 ---------------------------------------
 
 
 To use it in your Maven 2+ applications, you will need to add it as
 a dependency with:

     <dependency>
       <groupId>org.osscolib</groupId>
       <artifactId>osscolib-atomichash</artifactId>
       <version>{version}</version>
       <scope>compile</scope>
     </dependency>
